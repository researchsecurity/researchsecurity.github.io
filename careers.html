We’re Not Hiring—For Now

We appreciate your interest in joining Emergency Response Security Group (ERSG). At this time, we do not have any open positions. Our team is fully staffed and we are not actively recruiting for new roles.

Stay Connected

If you would like to be considered for future opportunities, you are welcome to send a brief note and résumé to careers@ersecurity.com. While we cannot respond to every inquiry, we will keep qualified submissions on file for six (6) months and review them as openings arise.

Our Work Culture

ERSG is dedicated to safeguarding our clients with integrity, innovation, and professionalism. We foster a culture of continuous learning, mutual respect, and a shared commitment to excellence. Should positions become available, we will post detailed role descriptions and application instructions on this page.

Thank you for your interest in ERSG careers. Please check back periodically for updates, and thank you for supporting Emergency Response Security Group.
