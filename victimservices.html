Victim Services

Last updated: July 1, 2025

Our Commitment

Emergency Response Security Group is committed to assisting victims of crime and stalking by offering free digital forensics services. Eventually, this service will be transitioned into a non‑profit initiative. Our goal is to empower victims with the tools and support they need for justice.

What We Offer

Our team has a proven track record of collecting and cataloging digital evidence, providing comprehensive reports that law enforcement can easily act upon. Our core services include:

  • Comprehensive digital forensics analysis of all digital assets
  • Evidence collection from digital devices
  • Expert analysis and interpretation of digital data
  • Preparation of detailed, organized reports for law enforcement

Initial Consultation & Process

The initial consultation involves meeting with you to discuss potential evidence and your current attack landscape. We will collect digital assets and begin analysis. This first pass helps create a catalog of evidence that makes it easier for law enforcement—who are often overburdened by caseload—to see the full scope of the situation.

Our team will work closely with you to:

  • Gather necessary digital evidence
  • Analyze the collected data to uncover critical information
  • Deliver a detailed, easy‑to‑understand report for law enforcement

Get Help

If you or someone you know is a victim of crime and has digital or technical assets involved, don’t hesitate to reach out for a free forensics consultation. We are here to provide valuable analysis and documentation to help you achieve justice.
